## SERP Patterns (What Google Rewards)

1. **Concrete benchmark data** — Every top-ranking page cites the 6.6% cross-industry median CR (Unbounce 2024 report). Industry breakdowns (SaaS 3.8%, ecommerce 4.2%, entertainment 12.3%) appear across positions 3–6.
2. **Structural clarity** — Lists with exact counts (5 traits, 10 elements, 8 steps) dominate. Readers scan before committing.
3. **Real conversion data** — Unbounce shows actual CRs per example (46.94% Promo, 77.38% College Board). CXL shows zero CR data.
4. **FAQ sections** — FormAssembly, Shopify, and Crazy Egg all include FAQ blocks that capture featured snippets ("What is a good conversion rate?", "How many CTAs?", "Should navigation be removed?").
5. **Mobile-first coverage** — Dedicated sections appear at positions 4, 5, 6. Mobile is framed as mandatory, not optional.
6. **Landing page SEO guidance** — Mailchimp dedicates a full section to LP-specific SEO (keywords, HTML structure, image alt, load speed). None of the others ignore this.
7. **Audit/improvement methodology** — Crazy Egg distinguishes between checkup audits and troubleshooting audits. Competitors cover how to diagnose underperforming pages.
8. **Psychological frameworks** — Shopify names cognitive levers explicitly: urgency, sunk cost fallacy, framing effect, halo effect.
