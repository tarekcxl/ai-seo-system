# SEO Quality Check

A skill for evaluating and improving content quality based on SEO best practices, EEAT principles, and user experience.

This skill ensures content is not only optimized, but also trustworthy, useful, and competitive.

---

## What this skill does

Use this skill to:

- evaluate content quality
- assess EEAT signals
- validate search intent alignment
- improve structure and readability
- ensure content is clear, useful, and credible

---

## When to use this skill

Use this skill when the user wants to:

- review content before publishing
- improve content quality
- ensure SEO best practices are applied
- check if content is competitive
- validate credibility and usefulness

---

## Core principles

- Content must satisfy search intent first
- Clarity is more important than complexity
- Trust and credibility influence rankings
- Structure improves both readability and SEO
- Useful content outperforms long content

---

## Evaluation framework

### 1. Search Intent Match
Check:
- does the content directly answer the query?
- is the format aligned with intent?

---

### 2. Content Depth
Check:
- are all important subtopics covered?
- is the content complete enough to compete?

---

### 3. Structure and Readability (GEO)
Check:
- clear headings (H2, H3)
- logical flow
- scannable format (short paragraphs, lists)

---

### 4. Clarity and Usefulness
Check:
- are explanations clear?
- does the content solve the problem?

---

### 5. EEAT (Credibility)

Check for:

- signs of expertise
- clear explanations and reasoning
- trustworthy tone
- use of examples or real insights

---

### 6. Differentiation

Check:
- is the content better than competitors?
- does it offer unique value?

---

## How to work

### Step 1: Evaluate content
Go through each dimension systematically.

---

### Step 2: Identify weaknesses
Focus on gaps that impact performance.

---

### Step 3: Suggest improvements
Provide specific, actionable changes.

---

## Output structure

### Quality score (optional)
High / Medium / Low

### Key issues
Main weaknesses affecting performance

### Improvements
Specific actions to improve quality

---

## Output quality bar

Strong evaluation:
- identifies real issues
- explains why they matter
- provides actionable fixes

Weak evaluation:
- generic feedback
- vague suggestions
- no clear direction

---

## Boundaries

This skill evaluates and suggests improvements.

It does not:
- rewrite full content
- perform keyword research
- define strategy

---

## Working style

Be precise and practical.

Focus on improvements that impact performance.
